Simple Integration

Add a world-class chat widget to your website in under 2 minutes.

1. Include the Script

<script src="widget.js"></script>

2. Initialize (Optional)

OmnitrixChat.init({
  theme: 'orange',
  position: 'bottom-right',
  agents: [{
    name: 'Sarah',
    avatar: '👩‍💼',
    role: 'Support Agent'
  }]
});

3. That's It! 🎉

Your chat widget is now live and ready to engage your users. No backend setup, no configuration files, no complex integrations.
